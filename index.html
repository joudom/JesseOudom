About Me

Father + Sports Addict + Foodie + Traveler

I'm a proud father of two extraordinary boys that are the catalysts for everything I do. On weekends, I'm likely at their soccer games doing the soccer dad thing. Speaking of athletics, if I'm not working, you can catch me watching some kind of sport - soccer, football, curling - whatever. I've naturally gained a healthy bit of competitiveness as a result, but who doesn't like winning? I also like to travel. It's been something I've grown accustomed to doing throughout my childhood. I was born in Florida, raised in Georgia, lived in Nevada, and graduated from high school in California. One of the benefits of moving around so early in life was the exposure and appreciation for all walks of life...and of course the different types of food out there! I'm always up for trying something new.


This actually brings me to this point in my journey. Making a mid-life career change was a very intimidating decision, but it's one I'm glad I made - both for my family's sake and also my own personal growth. By commiting to DigitalCraft's 26-wk immersive bootcamp, I've proven to myself that it's never too late and that I CAN have an impact on my future. Being able to create is an art in itself. As I continue to learn and grow as a developer, I hope to inspire and express myself by creating applications that bridge the gap between imagination and reality.
